Ello.

My name is Josh. I love learning. This site is mostly just bits and pieces from my process. It’s my practice - what I’m reading, thinking; my photos and scribbles.

By day I am a data journalist at The Guardian. Before that I covered economics, tech and made some radio for other outlets.

This site is over a decade old. It began as a book blog but morphed into a kind of scrapbook. It has gone through many stylistic iterations and platforms. As a result some links, line breaks, quotes etc. may be broken. The current version was created using the Eleventy static site generator.

You can also find me on Twitter, Micro.blog, Goodreads and GitHub.
