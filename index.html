Ello.

My name is Josh and I like to learn stuff. I am also a data journalist. Before that I was an economics editor, technology journalist and executive producer of a national current affairs radio show.

You can find most of my work at (The Guardian)[https://www.theguardian.com/profile/josh-nicholas]. I post a bunch of data on GitHub. You can also follow me on Goodreads.

This blog is a decade old now. It began as a place to post book reviews but has slowly morphed into a kind of scrapbook. A couple of years ago I added a section for photos and, more recently, scribbles.

Over the years this site has gone through many stylistic iterations and been ported to several different platforms and blogging tools. Some links, quotes etc. may be broken. The current version was created using Eleventy, is hosted on GitHub pages and uses a heavily modified fork of the base blog template.

If you'd like to get in touch I'd recommend Twitter.
